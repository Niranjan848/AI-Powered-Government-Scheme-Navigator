# Demo Flow

## Scenario 1: Farmer

1. Farmer logs in and opens the dashboard.
2. The assistant collects crop, land, location, and income details.
3. The system finds relevant schemes and explains eligibility.
4. A checklist is generated and stored.
5. The farmer returns later and the assistant remembers prior context.

## Scenario 2: Student

1. Student signs in and asks for scholarship options.
2. The assistant recommends scholarships based on education, category, and state.
3. The system explains why each scholarship fits.
4. The student downloads the checklist and saves the best option.

## Scenario 3: Woman Entrepreneur

1. Entrepreneur asks to compare Mudra Loan and Stand-Up India.
2. The assistant highlights differences in eligibility, benefits, and process.
3. The system recommends the best fit with a transparent explanation.
