# Pitch Deck Outline

## Slide 1: Problem Statement

Government scheme discovery is fragmented, jargon-heavy, and hard to act on.

## Slide 2: Existing Challenges

Users must search multiple portals, read long PDFs, and still miss eligibility details.

## Slide 3: Proposed Solution

An AI assistant that retrieves schemes, reasons over eligibility, and generates a checklist.

## Slide 4: AI Agent Workflow

Shows the LangGraph loop for memory, clarification, retrieval, reasoning, and persistence.

## Slide 5: System Architecture

Shows frontend, backend, agent, vector search, database, and memory stores.

## Slide 6: Technology Stack

React, FastAPI, LangGraph, PostgreSQL, Redis, FAISS, Gemini-compatible models.

## Slide 7: Live Demo

Farmer, student, and entrepreneur scenarios with personalized recommendations.

## Slide 8: Government Benefits

Higher scheme reach, faster awareness, better application readiness, and improved trust.

## Slide 9: Future Scope

Regional languages, portal integrations, mobile channels, and broader state coverage.

## Slide 10: Thank You

Closing with impact and deployment readiness.
